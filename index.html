Our Approach

Charette Cosmetics Medical Spas feature the most scientifically advanced beauty, anti-aging, and skin treatments to address every concern while enhancing your self-confidence and commitment to self-care.

We offer various popular aesthetic services ranging from fillers and injectables to chemical peels, lasers, microneedling, customized facials, body contouring, and more.

Charette Cosmetics has your skincare needs covered head to toe. We strongly believe in the benefit of customizing aesthetic treatments since each client’s skin care needs are different. Tailoring our treatments to your precise needs allows for the best results possible.

At the helm of Charette Cosmetics is Paul Charette, a master medical aesthetician, and cosmetic practitioner. Paul’s extensive aesthetic education, unparalleled techniques, and attention to detail are evident in every aspect of Charette Cosmetics. A skin rejuvenation expert dedicated to finding the most advanced and effective skincare products and protocols, Paul is passionate about helping his clients achieve their goals so they feel confident and beautiful in their skin.
